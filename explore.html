Vagamon

" 1100 meters above sea, far way from the loud city, lush greenery and serenity to gift every explorer, Vagamon could be your peaceful hill station in Kerala away from maddening city crowds. "
Vagamon Meadows : Vagamon's lush green grassland, just there, surrounded by Vagamon's hills, greenery, cool breeze that will take away your worries and tension as they blow through you.
Pine Hill : There's nothing to see in this place but a lot to feel ' Walking in the forest ' is a common English phrase and you'll actually walk into the woods heading to the Pine Hills.
Kurisumala : A common Christian pilgrimage, particularly during the week of Good Friday, it is flocked by followers. His followers come from different parts of the country with wooden crosses to climb up the hills. Kurisumala is one of Vagamon's most visited tourist spots.

Windy Walk

" Vakavanam windy walk trekking is a beautiful hike through the grasslands and shola forests of Vagamon hills. "
Windy walk is serene and gives you amazing views. We can explore the catchment area of Idukki reservoir and the vast stretches of green hills going in all directions.Elephants are most common, it's lucky you might encounter some wildlife. During the monsoon, the western ghats receive a special beauty, the Vagamon hills are no different, which is the perfect time to explore.
Windy walk oriented trekking program at vakavanam conducted by edc..under Kerala forest department.
Jeep trekking facilities provided along with goverment guide.

Thookkupalam (Hanging bridge)

" The environment is at its peak filled with nature's beauty. One of Kerala's longest hanging bridges."
The Ayyapancoil Temple and the hanging suspension bridge are a stunning tourist spot in the Mattukatta district of Idukki, with a glorious 300-year past. The temple and the bridge are major attractions around the area around the Periyar River.
The surrounding region has such glorious beauty that in its captivating glory you would be lost. Bridge is built across the Periyar River, connecting the Ayyappancoil and Kanchiyar in Idukki district, Kerala.
Raft boat made of bamboo and catamaran rides though not commercialized in the region are available and used by the locals.

Anjuruli

" Long circular tunnel that carries water to Idukki reservoir. Catchment area of Idukki Arch Dam, providing a bewitching and awesome view of nature."
The name Anchuruli literally means 'five vessels'. It is associated with the five small hills which are in the shape of inverted vessels and are visible when the water level in Idukki reservoir reduces.

Uluppuni

" Uluppuni's main attraction is the picturesque meadows. The journey to Uluppuni would be a very good trekking experience and it would be worth trying to see the majestic scenery."
Uluppuni in Idukki is the best place to soothe your over-strained mind and body with amazing green meadows all around. If you come together for a weekend with family or friends to enjoy Mother Nature's amazing beauty, you'll feel like you're on cloud nine.
It is one of the best off-road sites and in Idukki it is known to be a paradise for trekkers. If you're a rider on the off-road who would love to dive headlong into adventure, this one is for you.
Uluppuni is a part of the Western Ghats and, as mentioned above, located near Vagamon. It is situated around 9.4 km from Vagamon, towards the Pullikkanam – Elappara Road from Vagamon.
